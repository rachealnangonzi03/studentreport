Life Path Number 7

Life Path Number 7 General Characteristics

 

General Characteristics – Born to seek the Truth

If you are born under this Life path number, you have a clear sense of yourself as a spiritual being. As a result, you are devoted to investigating the unknown and finding the answers to the mysteries of life.

You feel that you have all the necessary tools to handle your task, because you possess a fine mind. Your thinking is predominant analytical and you are capable of great concentration and theoretical insight. Life path 7 is attracted by intellectual puzzles, enjoying the research and reunion of the small pieces in one single picture. Once you have sufficient puzzle items, you are able to develop a highly creative insight and practical solutions to problems.

Life Path number 7, Enjoy your own space

Number 7 in Numerology, prefers to work alone and also enjoys solitude. This life path number needs time to contemplate its ideas, without the influence of the collective consciousness. You are a loner, a person that has his own methods and ideas, even if they are different from the society`s templates. Close association, like marriage will give you headaches, because of your need for privacy. The opposite can cause you irritation and frustration

Learn to stay in balance

When all your life aspects are in balance, you are charming and attractive. You can become easily the spirit of a party. Especially relevant, you enjoy presenting your knowledge, fact that makes you attractive to the opposite sex.

You have the tendency to float from positive to negative feelings. You need to live your life at it`s full potential, and to learn from your own experiences.

Life path 7 need to be careful to the negative aspects that come with it, like selfishness and being moody.
